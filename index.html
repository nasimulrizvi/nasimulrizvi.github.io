Who I Am

About Me

A brief story about my journey, roots, and aspirations.

I am Md. Nasimul Islam Rizvi, a passionate technology researcher and lifelong learner born in Moktarpur, Kaliganj, Gazipur, Bangladesh. Currently, I am pursuing my undergraduate degree in Sociology (Honours, 1st Year) at Bhawal Badre Alam Government College.

Since childhood, I have been deeply fascinated by the intersection of technology and society. My interests span Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cybersecurity, and modern Web Development — areas where I constantly pursue self-directed learning beyond the classroom.

From 2017 to 2022, I actively participated in the Scout Movement, which instilled in me the values of leadership, teamwork, discipline, and patriotism. I have also completed multiple self-development courses from 10 Minute School, covering communication skills, public speaking, and professional email writing.

My father, Md. Aliullah, is an Assistant Professor, and my mother, Noor Akter Khanom, has always been my greatest supporter. Their guidance inspires me to remain ethical, responsible, and self-reliant.

My ultimate goal is to create impactful technological solutions for real-world problems and contribute positively to my community and country.
